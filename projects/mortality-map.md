### Overview

An exploration of global mortality data, presentated using various geospatial data visualisations. Mortality Map was started before COVID-19, but due to the influence of the pandemic upon the [World Health Organisation (WHO) mortality database](https://www.who.int/data/data-collection-tools/who-mortality-database), the project is undergoing a redesign.
