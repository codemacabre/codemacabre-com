I was invited to speak at [Wales TUC](https://www.tuc.org.uk/wales)'s workers and new technologies event at the Senedd in November 2021, on behalf of [United Tech and Allied Workers (UTAW)](https://utaw.tech).

Adapted from a draft of my article, _The Myth of the Smart Worker in the Data-Driven Workplace_, I highlighted the problematic mythologies of AI and automation in the workplace, and the very real inequalities they can create.
